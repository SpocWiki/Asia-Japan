> Yokohama (横浜町, Yokohama-machi) is a town located in Aomori Prefecture, Japan. 
> 
> As of 1 March 2023, the town had an estimated population of 4,258 in 2134 households, 
> and a population density of 33 persons per km2, in 1,779 households.
