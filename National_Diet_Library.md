## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The National Diet Library (NDL) (国立国会図書館, Kokuritsu Kokkai Toshokan) 
> is the national library of Japan and among the largest libraries in the world. 
> 
> It was established in 1948 for the purpose of 
> assisting members of the National Diet of Japan (国会, Kokkai) 
> in researching matters of public policy. 
> The library is similar in purpose and scope to the United States Library of Congress.
>
> The National Diet Library (NDL) consists of two main facilities in [[Islands~Japan/Honshu/Kantō/prefectures~Kantō/Tokyo|Tokyo]] and [[Islands~Japan/Honshu/Kansai/prefectures~Kansai/Kyoto,Prefecture|Kyoto]], 
> and several other branch libraries throughout Japan.
>
